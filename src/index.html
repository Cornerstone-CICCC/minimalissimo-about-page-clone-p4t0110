The Minimalissimo newsletter is sponsored by aprile, the hanging chair.

Minimalissimo is an independent print and digital magazine founded in 2009 and is dedicated to minimalism. Principally led by Carl Barenbrug for over 10 years and latterly by Manu Moreale, we showcased a curated selection of the finest examples of art, architecture, interiors, furniture, lighting, and product design. Minimalissimo will continue to exist as a digital archive with over 15 years of material to browse.
